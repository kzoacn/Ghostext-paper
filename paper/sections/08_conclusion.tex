\section{Conclusion}
Ghostext shows that a security-first local-LLM steganography pipeline can provide deterministic recoverability and explicit fail-closed behavior when Alice and Bob remain strictly synchronized and the Censor is passive. The ``almost perfect'' claim in this paper is deliberately scoped to that setting and to current implementation evidence. The next step is to extend evidence toward active-channel robustness and detector-focused evaluation while preserving the same reproducibility discipline.
