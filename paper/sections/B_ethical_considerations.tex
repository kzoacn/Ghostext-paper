\section{Ethical Considerations}
This work studies covert communication mechanisms and therefore has dual-use potential. Benefits include understanding design boundaries for secure and reproducible steganographic systems; risks include misuse for evasion. We mitigate risk by:
\begin{itemize}
\item clearly scoping threat model and non-goals,
\item avoiding unsupported claims of undetectability,
\item emphasizing fail-closed behavior and explicit boundary conditions,
\item releasing artifacts in line with responsible disclosure and conference policy.
\end{itemize}
